We’re pleased to announce that Pashen Bars are launching an updated size, packaging, and lower retail price! We’ve been working hard on this update over the last few months, and a lot of thought and planning has gone into this change.

New Benefits to you:

  • Retail price lowered from $3.99 to $2.99/bar! Horray!
  • New, easy-to-open, fin seal packaging - Let’s face it; the old bars were so hard to open. You can put that scissors away for good as our new bag has notches that make it super easy to open.
  • Slightly smaller, but perfect snack size. Same great flavor, taste and quality ingredients
  • New look for our Original flavor bar, allowing for easier differentiation between the flavors.

Our goal with the product and packaging changes is to maintain an exceptional degree of quality while also making Pashen Bars more affordable to you!

Thank you for your continued support and help as we transition. Please let me know if you have any questions or concerns.

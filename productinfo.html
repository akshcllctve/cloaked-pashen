ingredients and health benefits

Pashen bars are made using only the highest quality freshest raw and organic ingredients. Our ingredients are living foods. They are sprouted and dehydrated to maximize nutritional content. Ingredients include buckwheat, pumpkin seeds, sunflower seeds, cranberries, goji berries, black and white sesame seeds, honey, coconut oil, almond butter and cacao, all raw and organic using the perfect amount of cinnamon and vanilla. Pashen bars are loaded with flavor and nutriton.

organic almonds

Almonds are rich in potassium, dietary fiber and magnesium to support a healthy heart and immune system.

organic pumpkin seeds

Pumpkin seeds are a near perfect food - they have amino acids and unsaturated fatty acids, contain many B vitamins, as well as C, D, E and K. They also have wonderful minerals like calcium, potassium, phosphorous and niacin.

organic sesame seeds

Sesame seeds are a very good source of manganese and copper as well as calcium, magnesium, iron, phosphorus, vitamin B1, zinc and dietary fiber.

organic dried cranberries

Cranberries contain a source of phytochemicals known as condensed tannins - this means these berries are powerful antioxidants with an anti-inflammatory effect.

raw and organic cacao

Cacao is a raw chocolate from the rainforest that many nutritional experts believe to be a superfood. Superfoods have antioxidants that are nearly off the charts in comparison to other foods. Most importantly, pure chocolate is believed to stimulate various neurotransmitters such as serotonin.

raw and organic goji berries

The goji berry is well known for its high antioxidant properties. Goji berries are rich in many nutrients, minerals and amino acids.
